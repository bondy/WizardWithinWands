Younger Children (and older adults too)
Wands are only as safe as the user of the wand. Young children (those under the age of 5) may have wands but they should be supervised while playing with wands due to the inherent risk of poking oneself or another. The elderly may also need supervision due to their super human magic capabilities that can only be acquired after a long and illustrious life.

Ace a Speling (aka Spelling) Test

Serves 1
A great way to help you put your eyes before your ees and so on.

Ingredients:

  • 1 Wizard Within Wand.
  • 1 sheet of college or standard ruled loose leaf paper.
  • #2 pencil, black pen, or blue pen.
  • 1 brain filled with positive thoughts.

Directions:

  1. Place notebook paper on hard surface.
  2. Lay writing utensil on paper.
  3. Optionally place spelling list upon notepaper.
  4. Think positive thoughts about perfect spelling.
  5. Tap wand 3 times on paper and recite the following:
    • By all the power of 3 times 3, I can spell as it shall be.
      I will put I's before E's.
      I will note if it's an it's or an its.
      I welcome your testing of my wits.

Get Your Dream Job

Serves 1
If you desire to impress your friends or boss then cast this spell. However, following the directions and making NO substitutions is a must.

Ingredients:

  • 1 Wizard Within Wand.
  • At least one token representing the job you want. For example, if you want a bookkeeping job, then perhaps a calculator. If you're aiming for a Hollywood acting role, perhaps a DVD of a type of movie in which you would like to star.
  • Coins such as quarters, nickels, dimes, and pennies.
  • A bowl capable of holding the coins and the token.

Directions:

  1. Place coins and tokens in bowl and mix by hand.
  2. Tap bowl three times with Wizard Within Wand and recite the following:
    • By all the power of 3 times 3, I wish the following for me.
      To do the work I want.
      To get paid for the work I want.
      Let me not be for want.

World Peace

Serves billions with leftovers
Add a little magic to your desire for world peace.

Ingredients:

  • 1 Wizard Within Wand.
  • Mixing bowl.
  • Handful of flour, dirt or sand.
  • Green colored stuff (grass, food coloring, paint).
  • Blue colored stuff (flowers, sport drink, paint).
  • Figurines or sticks.

Directions:

  1. Recite the following while performing the following actions:
    • I place the earth (and toss sand, dirt, or flour into bowl)
    • I place the plants (and toss the green items into the bowl)
    • I place the water (and toss the blue items into the bowl)
    • I place the animals and humans (and toss the figurines/sticks into the bowl)
    • Run Wizard Within Wand 3 times around the rim of the bowl and say "Let them be."
